Gift Acceptance & Independence Policy

Protecting the Scientific Integrity of the Foundation

This policy protects the scientific integrity of the Science and Freedom for Everyone Foundation and governs the acceptance and management of gifts and contributions.

Statement of Independence

The Foundation accepts financial support from individuals, foundations, and corporate partners to further its mission of scientific literacy. However, the Foundation maintains absolute autonomy over its research agenda, data analysis, and publication of findings. Acceptance of a gift does not constitute an endorsement of the donor's views or business interests.

Non-Interference

No donor shall have the right to review, edit, or influence the findings of the Scientific Advisory Board (SAB) prior to publication. Our "Evidence Engine" is guided solely by peer-reviewed data and clinical integrity.

Transparency

All research published by the SAFE Research Institute will include a standard disclosure confirming that the researchers have no financial conflicts of interest related to the specific subject matter of the report.

Researcher Independence Statement

To ensure the absolute integrity of our "Evidence Engine," all members of the SAFE Scientific Advisory Board (SAB) serve as independent experts. All research findings are developed through independent review processes and are not influenced by external funders or industry stakeholders.
