SAFE: Code of Integrity & Confidentiality Agreement

Organization: Science And Freedom for Everyone Action (SAFE)

Position: Volunteer Stakeholder / Task Force Member

Preamble

As a member of the SAFE coalition, I recognize that our influence with lawmakers and the public depends entirely upon our reputation for scientific accuracy, nonpartisan objectivity, and professional integrity. To protect the mission of Science And Freedom for Everyone organization, I agree to the following standards:

1. Commitment to Scientific Integrity
  • Evidence-Based Advocacy: I agree to base all advocacy, testimony, and communications on the "SAFE Standard" — relying on peer-reviewed data and the prevailing scientific consensus.
  • Accuracy: I will not knowingly misrepresent scientific data, cherry-pick statistics, or use inflammatory rhetoric that undermines the credibility of the organization.
  • The Peer-Review Process: I understand that all "Model Evidence Briefs" must undergo SAFE's internal review process before being released publicly. I will not distribute draft versions as an official SAFE policy.
2. Confidentiality & Intellectual Property
  • Internal Strategy: I will maintain the confidentiality of SAFE's internal legislative trackers, strategy memos, and pre-publication research.
  • Proprietary Models: I acknowledge that the "Model Policy Analysis" frameworks and templates developed by SAFE are the intellectual property of the organization.
  • Non-Disclosure: I will not share internal communications (emails, Slack/Signal messages, or meeting minutes, etc.) with outside parties, including the media or opposing advocacy groups, without express written consent.
  • Sensitive Information: All personal, private, and sensitive information relating to employees — including but not limited to names, contact details, identification numbers, health information, and internal records — must be treated as strictly confidential.
3. Nonpartisanship & Representation
  • Issue vs. Party: I understand that SAFE is a nonpartisan organization. I will not use SAFE's resources, name, or my title within the organization to endorse political candidates or engage in partisan campaigning.
  • Clarification of Role: When speaking in a personal capacity or on behalf of another organization, I will clearly state that my views are my own and do not necessarily represent the official position of SAFE.
  • Model Talking Points: To ensure a consistent national message, I agree to adhere to Board-approved talking points when representing SAFE in an official capacity (e.g., legislative testimony, media interviews).
4. Conflict of Interest
  • Disclosure: I will promptly disclose to SAFE leadership any professional or financial affiliations that may present a conflict of interest regarding specific public health legislation.
  • Integrity of Influence: I will not use my position within SAFE to advance the private financial interests of myself, my employer, or any client.
5. Professional Conduct
  • Civil Engagement: In all interactions with lawmakers, the public, and opposing advocates, I will maintain a professional and respectful demeanor at all times.
  • Digital Responsibility: I will refrain from engaging in online harassment or spreading unverified information on social media platforms, recognizing that my digital footprint reflects on the SAFE organization.
  • This information and all associated SAFE assets — including but not limited to logos, templates, research data, and digital graphics — may not be shared, disclosed, distributed, accessed, or digitally altered by unauthorized individuals or third parties without explicit written permission. Any modification or unauthorized use of SAFE assets is strictly prohibited to ensure the integrity of our scientific messaging and nonpartisan brand.
6. At-Will Participation & Termination
  • Voluntary Relationship: I acknowledge that my participation in SAFE is at-will. Either I or SAFE may terminate this relationship at any time, with or without cause.
  • Survival of Confidentiality: My obligation to maintain the confidentiality of SAFE's internal strategies and proprietary data remains in effect even after my volunteer service ends.
